About the Project

The Garbage Detection System Using AI is developed by Pastors Mkondyo, a student from Midlands State University (MSU), and Anold Madzore, a student from the University of Zimbabwe (UZ). High rates of uncollected waste lead to various problems, including serving as breeding grounds for disease vectors, creating unpleasant environments, and posing health risks such as cholera, typhoid, dysentery, and malaria. Traditional reporting systems have proven ineffective due to the inability to verify reports accurately and determine precise locations.

This system allows citizens to report instances of uncollected waste by submitting images and location details. The AI then detects the presence of solid waste in the images, ensuring the validity of the reports. For further inquiries, contact Pastors Mkondyo directly via WhatsApp.

This system is beneficial for all local governing municipalities worldwide, offering a scalable solution to enhance waste management practices.
